KengTung

This out of the way region is rich in culture, breathtaking scenery and friendly people the adventure of a lifetime when we take you trekking to several hill tribe villages to see the different customs each tribe practices. Different beliefs of Animism, Buddhism and Christianity co-exist together in this remote town.

Attraction Places

  1. Hill Tribe Village
  2. Naung Tung Lake
  3. Pin Tauk Waterfall
  4. Kengtung Hot Springs
  5. Dhat Zoam Doi Monastery
